Cross-Border M&A Advisory

Cross-Border M&A Advisory

We support Japanese companies' overseas expansion and foreign companies' inbound investment, in collaboration with GAP, a global network of independent investment banks across 20+ countries. We bring local intelligence and an objective standpoint, with advice tailored to each transaction's phase and objective.

  • Outbound M&A by Japanese companies
  • Inbound M&A by foreign companies into Japan
  • Country-by-country market intelligence via GAP
  • Local partner introduction and due diligence support

Domestic M&A Advisory

Domestic M&A Advisory

We advise on strategic acquisitions and divestitures, business succession and MBOs, and group restructuring and carve-outs. We translate principal and advisor perspectives to structure transactions that work for both sides.

  • Strategic acquisition and divestiture advisory
  • Business succession and MBO support
  • Group restructuring and carve-outs
  • Structuring and tax optimization advisory

Independent Second Opinion

Independent Second Opinion

We provide neutral advice to boards, independent assessments of ongoing transactions, and re-examination of valuations. Free from product-sales or parent-firm conflicts, we support the quality and accountability of decision-making.

  • Neutral advisory to the board (fairness opinion supplement)
  • Independent assessment of ongoing transactions
  • Valuation re-examination
  • Presentation of strategic alternatives

Real Estate & Structured Finance

Real Estate & Structured Finance

  • Real estate-related M&A advisory
  • Real estate securitization advisory (REIT, CMBS, etc.)
  • Structured finance arrangement support
  • Real estate fund formation and GP coordination
